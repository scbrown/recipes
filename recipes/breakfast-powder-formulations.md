# Custom Breakfast Shake & Porridge Formulations

These recipes are optimized for a low glycemic index, high protein, and a "full and fluffy" mouthfeel. They use functional ingredients to emulate a milk-based texture using water and espresso.

## 1. The "Toasted Espresso" Travel Shake

Designed for portability and a "cake batter" flavor profile. This version omits Cream of Rice for easy mixing and low grit.

| Ingredient                | Amount          | Purpose                                          |
| ------------------------- | --------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Whey protein isolate      | 3/4 to 1 scoop  | Primary protein base                             |
| Egg white powder          | 1 tbsp          | Added "fluff" and structural stability           |
| Misugaru                  | 2 tbsp          | Roasted grain flavor                             |
| PB2 peanut powder         | 2 tbsp          | Nutty richness and protein                       |
| MCT powder                | 1 tbsp          | Satiety and creaminess                           |
| Milled chia seeds         | 1 tbsp          | Fiber and thickness                              |
| Banana flower powder      | 1 tbsp          | Prebiotic fiber and structural bulk (thickener)  |
| Creatine monohydrate      | 5 g (1 tsp)     | Performance and cellular energy                  |
| Sunflower lecithin powder | 1/2 tsp         | Emulsifier for silky texture                     |
| Xanthan gum               | 1/4 tsp         | Thickener (prevents separation)                  |
| Inulin & sea salt         | 1 tsp / pinch   | Prebiotic fiber and flavor enhancer              |

### Preparation

- **Dry blend:** Whisk all powders thoroughly. Use a blade grinder for the chia in short pulses to avoid heat.
- **Liquid:** Mix with 10–12 oz of water and a fresh espresso shot.
- **Temperature note:** If using egg white powder, ensure liquid is below 140 °F to prevent "scrambling."

## 2. The "Drinkable Porridge" (Home Version)

Includes Cream of Rice for a heavier, meal-replacement consistency.

| Ingredient            | Amount             | Purpose                                |
| --------------------- | ------------------ | -------------------------------------- |
| Cream of Rice         | 1.5 tbsp           | Starch base for "porridge" weight      |
| Misugaru              | 2 tbsp             | Nutty/toasted backbone                 |
| Whey protein isolate  | 1 scoop            | Protein content                        |
| Banana flower powder  | 1 tbsp             | Prebiotic fiber and structural bulk    |
| Creatine monohydrate  | 5 g (1 tsp)        | Performance and cellular energy        |
| MCT & lecithin        | 1 tbsp / 1/2 tsp   | Rich, milk-like mouthfeel              |

### Preparation

- Heat water/espresso to 150 °F.
- Whisk Cream of Rice, Misugaru, and banana flower powder into the liquid first.
- Let sit in a thermal flask for 3–5 minutes to soften the rice.
- Add remaining protein and thickeners last to maintain smoothness.

## 3. Flavor Modification Matrix

- **Cake batter:** Add butter extract and a pinch of citric acid.
- **Malted morning:** Add malted milk powder.
- **Vanilla cream:** Mix vanilla extract into the dry base blend.
