# Master Crepe, Pancake, and Nougat Recipes

A shelf-stable master mix that produces both pancakes and crepes, plus two nougat filling variations. Optimized for high protein and consistent texture.

## 1. Master Dry Mix Base

This shelf-stable base can be used for both pancakes and crepes.

- 6 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup potato starch
- 1 cup whey protein isolate
- 1.5 cups non-fat dry milk powder
- 0.5 cup allulose (or brown sugar)
- 0.25 cup MCT powder
- 2 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp xanthan gum

## 2. Crepe & Pancake Preparations

| Ingredient            | Pancake Ratio | Crepe Ratio       |
| --------------------- | ------------- | ----------------- |
| Master Mix            | 1.5 cups      | 1 cup             |
| Water                 | 1 cup         | 1 to 1.25 cups    |
| Melted Butter         | 2 tbsp        | 2 tbsp            |
| Baking Powder         | 2 tsp         | None              |
| Baking Soda           | 0.5 tsp       | None              |
| Egg Powder (optional) | 1 tbsp        | 2 tbsp            |

### Fresh Ingredient Variant (No Milk/Egg Powder)

If you are out of dry powders, use these substitutions for the wet mix:

- **Pancakes:** Use 1 cup fresh milk (replaces water/milk powder) and 1 large egg.
- **Crepes:** Use 1 cup fresh milk (replaces water/milk powder) and 2 large eggs for structural integrity.

## 3. Crepe-Only Master Mix (Pre-Mixed Powdered Eggs)

Use this variation to pre-mix an entire shelf-stable batch specifically scaled for crepes, incorporating the egg powder directly into the base.

- 6 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup potato starch
- 1 cup whey protein isolate
- 1.5 cups non-fat dry milk powder
- 1 cup + 4 tbsp egg powder
- 0.5 cup allulose (or brown sugar)
- 0.25 cup MCT powder
- 2 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp xanthan gum

### Preparation from Crepe-Only Mix

- 1 cup Crepe-Only Master Mix
- 1 to 1.25 cups water
- 2 tbsp melted butter

## 4. Crepe Nougat Fillings

### The Master Filling (Dense & Fudgy)

- 3 tbsp PB2 (powdered peanut butter)
- 1 tbsp egg white powder
- 1 tbsp potato starch
- 2 tbsp allulose (or brown sugar)
- 1 tbsp MCT powder
- 1.5 to 2 tsp water (adjust for desired density)

### Quick Fluff Nougat (Light & Spreadable)

- 2 tbsp egg white powder
- 1/4 cup warm water
- 1/4 cup allulose
- 2 tbsp PB2
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

### Fresh Variant (No Egg Powder)

Replace the egg white powder and water in the Master Filling with 1 tbsp of creamy peanut butter or Greek yogurt to act as the binder.

## 5. Technical Tips

- **Anti-rubbery texture:** Do not overmix. Let the batter sit for 5 minutes to allow the potato starch and xanthan gum to hydrate.
- **Consistency:** Crepe batter should be thin, similar to the consistency of water or very light cream.
- **Masking whey:** Add 1/4 to 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract to the wet ingredients to mask the protein scent.
